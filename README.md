# How to Play:

The objetive of the game is to have the least score by the end.
To do this, you replace the cards in your hand with lower value cards. If you make three in a row of a given card value horizontally or vertically, then you remove those cards from your hand, and thereby they do not grant you any points towards your score. 2's are considered wild, and can complete any a row/column for you. I.e. if your first row is A 2 A, it will clear. (Same for A A A, 2 2 2, A A 2, or any other such combination). You can only remove one row/column in a given turn.

Each turn, you may take one of three actions:

1. Flip over one of your face down cards (by clicking it)
2. Take the top card of the discard, and replace any of your cards with it
3. Take the top card of the deck, and replace any of your cards with it

The game ends one turn after any player reveals their whole hand.

Scoring:  
Each card in your hand counts towards your score, as follows:

A: 20 points  
2: 20 points  
3 through 10: Their value (i.e. 3 is 3 points, 4 is 4...)  
J: 10 points  
Q: 10 points  
K: 0 points  
Joker: -5 points
